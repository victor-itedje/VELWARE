About Us



Velware is a dynamic and innovative company, incorporated on the 20th of November, 2023. We were founded by a visionary entrepreneur: Mr Itedje Victor, incorporation with Mr Izuehie Sochima and Mr Samuel Osuagwu
who brought together their diverse skills and experiences to create a company that stands at the forefront of innovation, committed to delivering top-notch solutions to our clients.


Our Founder

Our Founder Mr Itedje Victor, alondside Mr Izuehie Sochima and Mr Samuel Osuagwu are not just our leaders, but the pillars that uphold our values and vision. Their leadership and dedication are the driving forces behind our success.


Our Vision



At Velware, we believe in the power of technology to transform lives and businesses. Our vision is to leverage this power to create solutions that are not only innovative but also practical and user-friendly.


Our Mission



Our mission is to provide our clients with the best possible solutions, tailored to their specific needs. We strive to exceed expectations, delivering high-quality products and services that give our clients a competitive edge.


Our Values



1. Innovation: Empasize on creating solutions that push the boundaries of technology. Encourage the employees to think outside the box and reward creative problem solving.
2. Integrity: Maintain transparency in all dealings, both internally and externally. Ensure that business is conducted honestly and ethically.
3. Collaboration: Foster a culture of teamwork where everyones ideas are valued. Promote open communication and cross-functional cooperation to achieve common goals.
Join us on our journey as we continue to innovate, inspire, and make a difference.
